Welcome to BrightnessJustWorks

BrightnessJustWorks is a macOS menu bar utility that routes the physical brightness keys (F1/F2) to whichever display your mouse cursor is currently on — built-in panel or external monitor.

This installer will place BrightnessJustWorks.app in /Applications and launch it automatically so you can grant Accessibility access right away.

Requirements: Apple Silicon Mac (M series), macOS 12 Monterey or later.
